Goals of the CS Compass App

Provide Timely Announcements
Keep CCS students informed with the latest updates and announcements from the council, ensuring that no important information is missed.
Display Upcoming Events
Offer an organized calendar of events so that students can easily view and keep track of upcoming activities and important dates within CCS.
Introduce Council Members
Allow students to get to know the members of the council, fostering a sense of connection and transparency within the school community.

our commitment



At CS Compass, we are committed to enhancing communication and engagement within the CCS community. Our primary goals are to ensure that CCS students are always up-to-date with the latest announcements from the council, so they never miss important information. We strive to keep students organized and informed by providing a comprehensive calendar of upcoming events, making it easy for them to track activities and key dates within CCS. Additionally, we are dedicated to fostering a sense of connection and transparency by introducing council members, allowing students to get to know the people who are working to support and represent them. Through these efforts, we aim to create a more informed and connected school community.
